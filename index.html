Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, artist development programs, and how Incord Entertainment can help advance your music career.

How can I join Incord Records?

We welcome submissions from talented artists. You can start by submitting your demo through our contact form, and our team will review your work and get back to you.

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive music production, artist development, digital distribution, marketing, and live event production services to help artists succeed in the industry.

How does digital distribution work?

We distribute your music to all major streaming platforms and digital stores worldwide, ensuring maximum reach and visibility for your releases.

What kind of support do artists receive?

Our artists receive dedicated support including career guidance, marketing assistance, technical support, and access to our network of industry professionals.

Do you offer artist development programs?

Yes, we offer comprehensive artist development programs tailored to help you grow as an artist, including vocal training, performance coaching, and brand development.

How do I book studio time?

You can book studio time through our online booking system or by contacting our studio management team directly through the contact form.
